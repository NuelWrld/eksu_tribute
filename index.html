Prof. Edward Olanipekun

The Vice Chancellor Of Ekiti State University

Professor Olanipekun is also a member of the Chemical Society of Nigeria and the Society of Environmental Toxicology and Chemistry. In 2017, Professor Olanipekun was appointed Chairman of the Governing Council of the Federal Polytechnic of Oil and Gas in Ekowe, Bayelsa State by President Muhammadu Buhari. He still holds this position today.

About

Prof. Edward Olanipekun's Biography

Professor Olanipekun was born in Ado-Ekiti on March 9th, 1966 and received his primary and secondary education at St. Paul's Anglican Primary School A and Mary Immaculate Grammar School, respectively. He then went on to earn his B.Sc in Chemistry from the University of Ibadan in 1986, followed by his M.Sc in Industrial Chemistry in 1988 and Ph.D in Industrial Chemistry in 1995, also from the University of Ibadan. After completing his education, Professor Olanipekun joined the faculty at Ekiti State University in October 1991. Over the years, he has held a variety of positions within the university, including Dean of the School of Postgraduate Studies, Director of the Directorate of Pre-Degree Programmes, Acting Head of the Department of Chemistry, and member of the University Governing Council,
